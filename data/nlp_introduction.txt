Introduction to Natural Language Processing

Chapter 1: Overview of NLP

Natural Language Processing (NLP) is a subfield of artificial intelligence that focuses on the interaction between computers and human language. The goal of NLP is to enable computers to understand, interpret, and generate human language.

Chapter 2: Main Tasks in NLP

NLP covers several core tasks, including:
1. Tokenization: Splitting text into words or subword units
2. Part-of-Speech Tagging: Labeling each word with its part of speech
3. Named Entity Recognition (NER): Identifying entities in text
4. Parsing: Analyzing the grammatical structure of sentences
5. Sentiment Analysis: Determining the sentiment of text
6. Machine Translation: Translating text from one language to another
7. Text Generation: Automatically generating text content

Chapter 3: Applications of NLP

NLP technology is widely used in various domains:
- Intelligent customer service systems
- Machine translation services
- Text summarization
- Voice assistants (e.g., Siri, Alexa)
- Search engine optimization
- Social media analysis

Chapter 4: History of NLP Development

NLP has gone through several stages:
1. Rule-based stage: Based on manually written grammatical rules
2. Statistical machine learning stage: Using statistical models and machine learning algorithms
3. Deep learning stage: End-to-end learning based on neural networks

Chapter 5: Modern NLP Technology

Modern NLP techniques are mainly based on deep learning, especially the Transformer architecture. The emergence of pre-trained models like BERT and GPT has greatly advanced the field of NLP.
